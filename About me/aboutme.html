Hello everyone,

My name is Romina and I am originally from Uruguay. I have been living in Wellington since 2014.

Something I have been thinking about values and beliefs is that everyone has their own ones. Sometimes we are able to relate to someone else's belief system but that is not something it will ever fully align since we all have different points of view. I have started exploring my personal belief system and trying to modify those things I think I should re-evaluate. It is probably a life-long journey but I personally believe that life on earth itself is a challenge that we decided to take when our soul came here. Something I do try to be mindful of is, being respectful to everyone since we all are on a different journey and have lived different experiences.

I am attending Dev Academy because I have recently become passionate about IT and how it is such a diverse field in which there is always something new to learn. I feel that Dev Academy has a wonderful curriculum that not just teaches us about becoming a professional programmer but also about seeing the person as a human being, developing our soft skills.
