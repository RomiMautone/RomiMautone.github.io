Neuroplasticity- Sunday 11th July 2021

Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people


Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity are highly important and beneficial for every single one of us.
As neuroplasticity research shows, human beings of any age are able to change, adapt and reorganize our brains.
Our neurons are alive, therefore our brain is malleable.
Explaining these principles, that we have the capacity to do so and we are fully in charge of our mental growth, encourages people to take a more active role in their learning process or recovery.


Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit


I had the opportunity to learn this powerful concept a while ago, I just wasn’t aware of its technical name. Also, I wasn’t familiar with the extent of this concept.
I have been working on something called “brain reprogramming” for self-improvement of my thoughts. Being in charge of our thoughts and how we perceive things and accept them or not, tell us a lot about our inner thinking.
Meditation is a great helper when reflecting over the realisations we encountered during that certain situation. This is where neuroplasticity tells us that we are able to achieve our highest potential, it is just a matter of believing in it.
I personally feel that I already implement several of the neuroplasticity principles on a daily basis in different areas of my life.


What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?


I feel that believing in it, believing that it is possible to alter our brain, that gives us motivation and the encouragement needed to start the action.
I guess another factor is to use it, use it, and repeat until you feel confident in whatever it is you are trying to achieve.
By just trying it, there are certain brain connections already happening that activate the path, and by repeating it you just make that connection even stronger.

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant


Growth Mindset is knowing that we can actually learn new things and improve ourselves and our capacity.
Growth mindset talks about seeing obstacles as challenges instead of a barrier as opposed to a “fix” mindset.
Growth mindsets is the idea of expansion and feeling the need or desire to learn something.
It is extremely relevant to every single one of us, because without a growth mindset, we won’t be able to self-improve, learn new things in life, face our obstacles among many others challenges.

I feel like I heard many of these concepts before but it has been very beneficial for me to have a reinforcement and understanding how it actually works.
I feel that having to be exposed to this subject makes my mind crave to learn even more in life.
